WIRELESS INTERNET SERVICES

The BoatYard provides free WiFi to its slip tenants via a dedicated T1 line.  Commercial tenants or outside parties may also log on as a paid user by utilizing our hotspot with SSID: “TBY Hotspot”  Just logon to our wifi conection and launch your browser.  You will be directed to either enter your password supplied by the BoatYard or you can enter your credit card information for immediate connection.
