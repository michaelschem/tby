Welcome to The BoatYard!

Located in beautiful Marina del Rey, California, The BoatYard is the Marina’s oldest full-service boat yard and marina.  At the BoatYard we are focused on providing the highest level of personal service to every customer.  In addition, we want to be your boating resource center where you can find answers to all of your marine questions.  Our senior managers are licensed USCG Captains with decades of boating experience worldwide.  Collectively, our staff has over a century of experience in servicing boats of all types.  Please stop by and check us out!  There’s always a pot of fresh coffee waiting for you.

Our Promise

We provide our customers with a one-stop boat yard and marina where all of your boating needs and questions can be addressed. We endeavor to provide the highest level of workmanship and service available in the Marina. It's that simple, the more convenient your boating is, the more you will enjoy it. It's our motto to "Do it right the first time". In addition, our sister property, Del Rey Landing, is your vital Marina del Rey resource for fueling, provisioning, and boat repairs for yachts up to 250 feet.
