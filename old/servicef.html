A Full Service Yard in the Marina!

Our boat yard offers a multitude of repair and maintenance services on pleasure crafts and commercial vessels up to 60 tons. We also feature a Do-It-Yourself Yard for boaters requesting to do repairs themselves. Our yard features two marine travel lifts, a 35-ton crane and a 60-ton crane. Our boat yard offers a multitude of repair and maintenance services on pleasure crafts and commercial vessels up to 60 tons and up to 18 ft wide.

Our comprehensive services include Haulout, Bottom painting, Custom racing bottoms, Rigging, Commissioning, Fiberglass & Gelcoat repairs and every other level of repair and service your sail or motor yacht requires. We have the right equipment for the job, operated by highly qualified journeyman mechanics. Or choose to do it yourself in our work yard. Call for pricing and yard rules.

Boat Signs

Do you need to add or replace your boat signs, boat graphics or boat lettering? The BoatYard does paint signs, graphics and lettering.

Do-it-yourself Yard

The Do it Yourself (DYS) Yard is designed for the boat owner to perform his/her own work and is only available to the boat owner and immediate family members. Subcontractors and other helpers are prohibited. As a full service boat yard we are here to help you with all of your repair and maintenance needs. Call our Service Dept. to answer any questions you have and to schedule your appointment.

* DYS customers need to bring in their boat title for proof of ownership.
