RACING BOAT STORAGE

Having served many successful race boats including ROSEBUD, REVOLUTION, EMPRESS, PENDRAGON , and BULL the BoatYard stands ready for even the most demanding racer.  Services and amenities available include:
  • Dock space for work or berthing
  • Transportation to and from MDR Yacht Clubs
  • Flexible Haul and Launch schedule in tailored to meet your needs
  • Weekend Haul and Launch available
  • Secure location and 24 hour video monitoring and recording
  • Abundant parking
  • Easy access to the ocean
  • Deep keel pit on the hard for keels up to 15 feet deep
  • Certified Clean Marina with on-site clarifier
  • Access to the most talented pool of shipwrights and mechanics in Southern California.
  • Rigging services

All racers are welcome at the BoatYard!  Please call for a quote or for additional services you may require during your racing schedule in Southern California.
